Animation Sound

My story tells the process of creation. As the artist juggles the light, the music repeats a juggling beat. As the instruments and tools of creation come down, the acoustic and electric guitars chimes into the melody alongside vocals. The notes get higher as the light of inspiration comes down through the instruments and towards the blank canvas that shines and reflects an egg rendering to symbolize the birth of creation. Notice how both sides of the process are different, yet are symmetrical all the same. No matter how old or how new and diverse the tools that we use to create, with them, we are still creating. One may provide more methods and data for creation, but they still serve the core purpose.
